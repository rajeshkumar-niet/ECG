About This Website

FixMyECG is a smart platform for ECG signal enhancement. ECGs often get corrupted due to interference, motion artifacts, or faulty sensors. This can lead to diagnostic errors. Our service uses smart algorithms to remove unwanted noise from ECG signals and produce clean, clinically relevant data.

With the growth of wearable tech and portable health devices, there's a huge surge in ECG recordings — not all of them are clean. That’s where FixMyECG comes in, providing a powerful yet easy-to-use platform to clean your ECG data before analysis.

What is ECG?

ECG (Electrocardiography) is a method used to record the electrical signals generated by the heart. It is widely used to detect heart problems and monitor heart health. An ECG can reveal abnormalities like arrhythmias, heart block, and signs of ischemia.

Accurate ECG readings are crucial for correct medical interpretation. Hence, clean signals are a must. That's why our tool aims to improve ECG signal quality using AI-powered filters.
